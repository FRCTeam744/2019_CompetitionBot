◆ FourbarHome()

void Fourbar::FourbarHome ( bool  homingBut)

Sets fourbar to home position.

Parameters
homingButTrue if homing the fourbar.

The fourbar needs to stow a little past the retracted limit switch. The homing routine takes care of slowly retracting the fourbar such that it can read the rising edge of the retracted limit switch and then lower speed to correctly reach the desired millimeters beyond that rising edge.
